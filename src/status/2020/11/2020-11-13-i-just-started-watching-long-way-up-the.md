I just started watching Long Way Up the other night, an electric motorcycle journey from the tip of South America, up to LA, with Ewan McGregor. Someone mentioned that not everyone watched Evel Knievel jumping the Grand Canyon to see him make it—some watched it to see if he WOULDN’T. I stopped the episode and YouTubed Evel and fell into a hole. I never knew the guy was such a character. Highly recommended if you’re up sick and need something strange but interesting to watch.
