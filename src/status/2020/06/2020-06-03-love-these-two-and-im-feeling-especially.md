Love these two, and I’m feeling especially appreciative and lucky to have such a wonderful partner to help navigate this life. Pax’s face in this photo cracks me up. I took some notes. I think we’ll all be happy when this virus business is over and we can feel safer going out more often (and maybe having a beer or two on a patio somewhere - Oregon isn’t the same without that).
